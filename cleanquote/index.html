How pros add this widget (Sellable v1):

1) You host cleanquote-widget.js once.
2) Pros paste this on their site where they want the button/card to appear.

<div id="cardinal-cleanquote-widget"></div> <script src="https://YOUR-FRONTEND-DOMAIN.com/widgets/cleanquote-widget.js" data-pro-name="BrightWash Pressure Pros" data-primary-color="#21c78b" data-contact-endpoint="https://YOUR-API.com/lead" ></script>

The script renders the same card your demo shows, branded with their company name. On quote submit, it POSTs the lead + quote details to your backend (or directly emails them).
